The Golden Chalice

Behold! The most magnificent, extraordinary, bewildering dessert the world has yet to taste! A rich, chocolate milkshake adorned with golden, glimmering treats! Learn how to concoct this chalice for champions in your own home!

Ingredients

  • 4 scoops of chocolate ice cream (Or your favorite ice cream!)
  • ½ cup of milk
  • Caramel syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Caramelized popcorn
  • Mini waffle cones
  • Chocolate bar
  • Pretzels
  • Straws

Instructions

  1. Place 4 scoops of ice cream into a blender
  2. Add ½ cup of milk to the blender
  3. Add a drizzle of caramel inhto the blender
  4. Cover your blender and blend until your mixture is smooth
  5. Drizzle caramel along the insides of a glass mug (Or your favorite container!)
  6. Pour the mixture into the mug
  7. Dispense whipped cream around the rim and on the top of your milkshake
  8. Garnish your milkshake by adding caramelized popcorn, mini waffle cones, and chocolate pieces
  9. Further garnish your milkshake by placing pretzels around the rim of the milkshake
  10. Add a straw and enjoy! (Best served cold)
